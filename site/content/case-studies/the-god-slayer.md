Renaissance worked with Pathea to announce their brand-new game The God Slayer. This consisted of direct 1-2-1 pitching of the trailer to media & content creators and distributing the relevant press releases, advising & supporting the running of VIP reveal sessions under embargo, pitching & securing all attendees as well as hosting sessions, plus we also helped in advising & crafting key product messaging & how trailers or video content should be utilised for maximum exposure & success.​



{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/god1.png" align="left"  alt="god1" width="532" height="296" processing="resize">}}

All the activities resulted in **328 articles** from **240 media outlets** worldwide with an estimated UVPM figure of **577.9 million** in the first 7 days. ​

​

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/god2.png" align="left"  alt="god2" width="530" height="356" processing="resize">}}

Highlights included **IGN US (75.7m), ScreenRant (35.1m), GameRant  (33.8m), TechRadar UK (18.3m) & Galaxus (18.3m)** to name just a few**.**​

​

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/god3.png" align="left"  alt="god3" width="457" height="352" processing="resize">}}
