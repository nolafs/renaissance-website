Renaissance worked with Genigods Lab to announce their brand-new game Genigods Nezha. To maximise the reveal announcement, we directly pitched the trailer to media & content creators on a 1-2-1 level, along with distributing any additional press releases. We also advised & supported the running of VIP reveal sessions under embargo, pitching & securing all attendees as well as hosting the  sessions, plus we also helped in advising & crafting key product messaging & how trailers or video content should be utilised for maximum exposure & success at this crucial time in the campaign.​

​

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/gen1.jpg" align="left"  alt="gen1" width="657" height="720" processing="resize">}}

All the activities resulted in **257 articles** from **199 media outlets** worldwide with an estimated UVPM figure of **211.2 million** in the first 7 days. ​

​

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/gen2.jpg" align="left"  alt="gen2" width="818" height="720" processing="resize">}}

Highlights included **TechRadar UK (15.1m), GameReactor (15m), 3DJuegos (7.7m), Gamingbible (7.1m) & Everyeye.it (6.9m)** to name just a few**.**​

​

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/gen3.jpg" align="left"  alt="gen3" width="613" height="704" processing="resize">}}

The game trailer was also distributed on both **IGN** & the **official PlayStation** YouTube channels & at the time of writing had reached a combined viewership of over **500,000** views.​
