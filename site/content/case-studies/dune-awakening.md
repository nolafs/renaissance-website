Renaissance led the European PR campaign for Funcom’s biggest ever IP, the survival MMORPG Dune Awakening, which is linked to the current Dune movie franchise/trilogy. From its announcement through to launch on PC, the work covered a 3-year period & was also in conjunction with local agency partners in both Spain & Italy. 

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/da1.png" align="left"  alt="da1" width="578" height="488" processing="resize">}}

Throughout the campaign Renaissance supported at numerous live events including The Game Awards, Summer GameFest & Gamescom along with other events, such as a bespoke hands-on at Funcom’s Olso studio & a dedicated film screening of Dune: Part 2 in LA.

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/da2.png" align="left"  alt="da2" width="515" height="464" processing="resize">}}

Campaign coverage since the games announcement saw a total of **3257 articles** with a combined reach of **40 billion UVPM**. The launch announcement achieved **389 articles** with a reach of **4.3 billion** & **53 reviews** within the first seven days.

{{< imageSize image="/images/case-studies/da3.png" align="left"  alt="da3" width="415" height="487" processing="resize">}}

Highlights included **IGN UK (117.4), Sapo TEK (56.3m), PCGamesN (13.5m), Areajugones (65.1m), TGcom24.it (39.6m) & PC Gamer UK (24.6m)** to name just a few.
